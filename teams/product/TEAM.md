# Product Team

The product team defines the roadmap, writes requirements, and validates outcomes.

## Responsibilities
- Roadmap prioritization
- Requirements writing
- User feedback synthesis
- Success metrics definition

## Note
This is a placeholder team. In a fully autonomous setup, product decisions may be driven by the board or delegated to an agent with product-management skills.
